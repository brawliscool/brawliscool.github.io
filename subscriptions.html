Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

You'll be billed on the same day each month. We accept all major credit cards and can set up automatic payments for your convenience.

Can I skip a week if I'm out of town?

Absolutely! Just let us know at least 24 hours in advance and we'll reschedule your appointment at no extra charge.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What if I need to cancel?

No problem! There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Just let us know and we'll stop your subscription at the end of the current billing period.
